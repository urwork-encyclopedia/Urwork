On colour · The Venetian legacy

El Greco trained in Venice under the influence of Titian. The teal-grey of the storm sky, the phosphorescent green of the hills, the pale luminosity of the architecture: these are Venetian colours applied to a Castilian subject. El Greco did not paint what he saw. He painted what he remembered, and what he imagined, in a Venetian light absorbed thirty years earlier.
